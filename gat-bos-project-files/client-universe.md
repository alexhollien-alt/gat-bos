# CLIENT UNIVERSE -- Who Alex Serves

All skills that reference "agents," "clients," or "partners" inherit these definitions.

---

## Tier 1 -- Core Revenue Clients (realtors and real estate agents)

The primary audience for all marketing, email, print, and BD outreach. These are the professionals whose transactions generate title and escrow orders. When a skill references "agents" or "clients" without qualification, it means this group.

## Tier 2 -- Referral Partners (lenders, loan officers, mortgage brokers, insurance agents, property managers)

These professionals sit inside the same transaction ecosystem. They do not generate title orders directly but they refer buyers to agents, appear on every closing, and send investors. A strong relationship with a lender means referrals two layers deep.
